Principal Investigator



Man-man Hu

Dr. Man-man (M.M.) Hu is currently an Assistant Professor of Civil Engineering at The University of Hong Kong leading the Hu Lab.

Dr. Hu holds a Ph.D degree from Duke University (USA) and a B.Eng degree from Zhejiang University (China). Prior to joining HKU, Dr. Hu worked as Vice Chancellor’s Research Fellow at UNSW, Sydney (Australia). Dr. Hu’s research work has been focusing on the interdisciplinary field of environmental geomechanics driven by modern-day geo-engineering activities and fundamental understandings of geomaterial behavior in response to environmental perturbations. Her current research group focuses on tackling the fundamental Multi-scale Multiphysics processes in geomaterials that play an essential role in a wide-context geoenvironmental circumstances concerning carbon neutrality and geo-engineering adaptation to Climate Change.
